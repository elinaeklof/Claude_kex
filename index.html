FAQ
Everything you need to know
What is Dual Input technology?
Dual Input is a patented technology (Swedish Patent Office PRV, application G06F3/0488 — "Apparatus and Method for Supporting Touch Input Events") that allows the cashier and the customer to interact on the same screen simultaneously without technical conflicts. For example, the cashier can apply a discount while the customer enters their email address — at the same time.
Do we need to change our existing POS system?
No. ID24 is designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing POS system without any modifications. The software runs on a separate device and communicates passively with your POS — no internal IT resources required for setup.
How long does it take to launch a pilot?
A pilot typically goes live within 8–12 weeks from sign-off. ID24 handles remote installation, software testing, and customization — all with full support included. No internal IT effort required on your side.
Which industries does ID24 serve?
Primarily retail, banking, and hotels. With 15+ years of experience and software installed in 20+ countries, ID24 is now bringing the same proven methodology into the financial services sector with a dedicated banking solution.
How does the cashier fraud detection work?
The AI anonymously analyzes transaction data — discounts, returns, error registrations, and cashier patterns — against industry benchmarks and custom algorithms. Store managers receive actionable insights in real time or via weekly reports.
What hardware is compatible?
Any consumer-grade Android, iOS, or Windows tablet works with ID24's software. For purpose-built solutions, the D|2 display range offers integrated NFC, front-mounted camera, and robust metal housing in 8", 10", 13.3", and 15.6" sizes. Adyen SFO1 and Wacom display lines are also supported.
